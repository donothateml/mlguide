Cross Validation

📌 Definition

Technique to evaluate model using multiple splits.

⚙️ How It Works

  • Split data into K parts
  • Train on K-1
  • Test on remaining
  • Repeat

🔢 Example

5-fold cross validation.

🎯 Interview

Why use cross validation?

Answer: Better evaluation

🧪 Practice

Cross validation reduces?

Answer: Overfitting risk
