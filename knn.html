K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)

📌 Intuition

KNN works by looking at nearest neighbors and taking majority vote.

⚙️ Steps

  1. Choose K
  2. Find nearest K points
  3. Majority vote

🔢 Example

K=3 → Red, Red, Blue → Output = Red

🎯 Interview

Q: What happens if K is too large?

A: Model becomes too smooth (underfitting)
