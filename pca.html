Principal Component Analysis (PCA)

📌 Definition

PCA reduces dimensions of data.

⚙️ How It Works

  • Find directions of maximum variance
  • Project data

🔢 Example

Reduce 3D data to 2D.

✅ Advantages

  • Reduces complexity

❌ Disadvantages

  • Loss of information

🎯 Interview

PCA is used for?

Answer: Dimensionality reduction

🧪 Practice

Answer: Decreases
