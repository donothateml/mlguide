K-Means Clustering

📌 Definition

K-Means is an unsupervised algorithm that groups data into clusters.

⚙️ Steps

  • Choose K
  • Assign points to nearest centroid
  • Update centroid
  • Repeat

🔢 Example

Group customers into 3 clusters.

✅ Advantages

  • Simple and fast

❌ Disadvantages

  • Need to choose K

🎯 Interview

What is centroid?

Answer: Center of cluster

🧪 Practice

K-Means is supervised?

Answer: No
